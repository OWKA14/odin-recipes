Nasi Goreng

Description

Hong Kong fried rice (often associated with Yang Chow or Cantonese style) is a typical Chinese fried rice dish that emphasizes a savory taste, smoky (wok) aroma, and a "clean" or light appearance, in contrast to Indonesian fried rice which tends to be sweet.

Ingredients

  • 150g rice
  • 1 carrot, chopped and boiled
  • 1 egg
  • 2 stalks spring onions
  • 4 onion
  • 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp mushroom stock
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Saut√© the onions, spring onions, and carrots until fragrant. Set aside, then add the eggs.
  2. Stir until combined, add the rice and add all the spices.
  3. Stir again until the spices are combined. You can adjust the seasoning here. Once it's to your liking, remove from heat.
  4. Hong Kong fried rice is ready to be served, warm is more delicious
