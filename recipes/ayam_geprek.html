Ayam Geprek

Description

Ayam geprek is a popular Indonesian dish, originating from Yogyakarta, consisting of crispy fried chicken pounded or crushed with spicy chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • 150gr White Rice
  • 100gr Fried Chicken
  • 5 Red Chili Peppers
  • 1 CLove Garlic
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Hot Oil
  • 3 Slices Cucumber
  • 2 Wonton Crackers
  • 200ml Sweet Iced Tea

Steps

  1. Deep-fry the battered chicken until cooked through and crispy, then shred it coarsely.
  2. ind the chilies, garlic, and salt, drizzle with hot oil, and stir well.
  3. Mix the fried chicken with the crushed chili sauce.
  4. Serve the white rice, crushed chicken, cucumber slices, and wonton crackers on a plate.
  5. Serve with sweet iced tea.
