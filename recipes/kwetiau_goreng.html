Kwetiau Goreng

Description

Fried kwetiau (rice noodles) is a dish of wide, flat, stir-fried rice flour noodles, popular in Indonesia and Malaysia (char kway teow). With a savory-sweet flavor and a chewy texture, this Chinese-Indonesian dish is typically cooked with sweet soy sauce, egg, meat (chicken or beef), shrimp, and vegetables such as mustard greens and bean sprouts.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of flat rice noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 meatballs
  • 5 pieces of bok choy
  • 1 handful of bean sprouts
  • 5 shallots
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of Cap Kapal salt
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of bouillon powder

Steps

  1. Heat the pan, pour in the oil and then saut√© the ground spices, namely shallots and garlic.
  2. Once the fried eggs smell fragrant, scramble them until cooked through. Then add the meatballs and stir well.
  3. Add the rice noodles, sweet soy sauce, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Add Cap Kapal salt.
  4. Add the ground pepper and stock powder and stir well. Then, add the bean sprouts and bok choy and cook until the spices are absorbed.
  5. Then remove and serve. Enjoy.
